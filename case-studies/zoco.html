Zoco

A B2B custom apparel venture serving corporate, event and sports clients.

Challenge

Build a flexible apparel model for clients needing event clothing, teamwear and corporate apparel.

Strategy

Focus on B2B orders, practical fulfillment, client communication and made-to-order execution.

Execution

Managed client requirements, vendor coordination, pricing, order management and delivery flow.

Results

Active venture with recurring B2B demand from sports, events and corporate apparel clients.
