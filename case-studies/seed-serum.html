Case Study

Seed & Serum

A haircare brand built around positioning, product direction, audience mapping and brand development.

Challenge

Create a consumer haircare brand with a clear identity, product promise and audience fit.

Strategy

Develop market positioning, target audience understanding, brand voice and product presentation.

Execution

Worked on branding, product packaging, content direction, customer communication and growth planning.

Results

Generated 2,000+ orders and PKR 1.3M+ in cumulative sales before moving into reformulation and rebranding.
