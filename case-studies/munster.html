Case Study

University of Münster Conference

Branding, collateral and editorial support for the Language & Power international academic conference.

Challenge

Create a consistent academic event identity across digital and print materials.

Strategy

Build a professional visual system for conference communication, publication and participant engagement.

Execution

Designed logos, brochures, schedules, certificates, digital assets and supported the Book of Abstracts.

Results

Delivered a complete branding and collateral system for an international conference with 50+ researchers and PhD scholars.
