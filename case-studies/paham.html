Case Study

Paham Limited

Growth and commercialization work for SaaS and ERP products sold through white-label and subscription models.

Challenge

Build market attraction, generate qualified leads and create routes to revenue for 10+ software products across multiple industries.

Strategy

Structured outreach, lead generation, sales funnels, partnership development, recruitment and sales pitch design.

Execution

Worked with direct and indirect teams to create leads, qualify prospects, build sales conversations and strengthen brand positioning.

Results

Closed 2 white-label clients, generated 15,000+ leads with the team, recruited 20+ people and built partnerships with multiple companies.
